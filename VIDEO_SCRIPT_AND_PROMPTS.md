- **Runway Gen-3**: Best for realistic scenes and complex animations
- **Pika Labs**: Great for abstract visualizations and UI animations
- **Stable Video Diffusion**: Good for consistent style across scenes

### Voiceover:
- **ElevenLabs**: Most natural AI voices (use "Professional" or "Tech" voice)

### Music:

- **DaVinci Resolve**: Professional, free

## PRODUCTION TIPS

1. **Generate scenes individually** - easier to control quality
2. **Use consistent prompts** - maintain visual style across scenes
3. **Add motion blur** - makes AI video look more professional
4. **Color grade everything** - ensure consistent black/green theme
5. **Use sound effects** - UI clicks, whooshes, data sounds
6. **Add subtle grain** - makes it feel more cinematic
7. **Sync to music beats** - transitions on beat drops
8. **Test voiceover pacing** - ensure it matches visuals

---

## EXPORT SETTINGS

- Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p) or 3840x2160 (4K)
- Frame Rate: 60fps for smooth motion
- Codec: H.264 (for web) or ProRes (for quality)
- Bitrate: 20-50 Mbps
- Audio: 320kbps AAC
